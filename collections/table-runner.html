          Table Runners

          A practical and ornamental table accessory, lay table runners across the length of the table to protect your lovely table. Or use shorter runners across the width, in sync with the placement of chairs. Protect and simultaneously decorate, or give an older table a face-lift. Pick from an array of handwoven banana stem, river grass, leaves and cotton yarn blend; gorgeous block printed cotton; handwoven cotton; handwoven flax linen; and handwoven silk!
