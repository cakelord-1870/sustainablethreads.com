          Napkins 

          Have a meal with elegantly designed, superior-quality, handcrafted napkins. Catch those crumbly crumbles or drips and dribbles, wipe your hands, dab your mouth and wash and reuse. Say goodbye to paper napkins for good! Use at formal dinners and celebrations, at picnics, or include with a packed lunch for your child, and last but not least, make a table come alive with napkins! Sizes range from  9"x9" for children or cocktail & tea parties, 16"x16", 18"x18" and 20"x20". Go for all-cotton or flax linen. Techniques used are hand weaving and hand block printing.
